Chile, 31 May - 19 June 1962 (Arica, Rancagua, Santiago, Vila del Mar).
Decided at FIFA Congress in Lisbon, 9 June 1956. Entires deadline 15
Dec 1959. 56 teams entered, 51 actually played (9 for the first time).
Qualifications drawn 28 Feb 1960 in Basel. Finals drawn 18 Jan 1962
in Santiago.
