USA, 17 Jun - 17 Jul 1994 (Boston, Chicago, Dallas, Los Angeles (Pasadena), New York,
Orlando, Pontiac (indoors), Stanford, Washington). Decided at
FIFA Congress in Zürich, 4 Jul 1988. Entries deadline 15 Sep 1991. 147
teams entered, 146 seeded, 133 actually played (16 for the first time -
JUG withdrawn due to UN sanctions). Qualifications drawn 8 Dec 1991 in New
York. Finals drawn 19 Dec 1993 in Las Vegas.
