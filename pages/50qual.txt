== Europe

<!-- start pre -->

17 teams entered (including ISR and SYR), ITA exempted as defending champion.
AUT and BEL were withdrew after seeding. TUR withdrew after playing one
match. 7 places in 6 groups, one group of 4 teams (with 2 places), rest -
2-3 teams (exception group 5, where preliminary matches were played).

== South America

8 teams entered, including exempted BRA as host nation. ARG, ECU, PER
withdrew, so no matches at all were played in this zone (the third WC
qualifications in a row - quite an unusual situation).

Group 7 [Bolivia, Chile]  ‹§gr7›

26.02. La Paz      BOL-CHI 2:0 (0:0) Mena 52, Ferrer 64 (Alvarez BOL)

As ARG withdrew, CHI and BOL had walkovers and the rest of ther programme
was scratched.

Group 8 [Uruguay, Paraguay]  ‹§gr8›

PER and ECU withdrew, URU and PAR had walkovers.
