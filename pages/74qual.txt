== Europe

<!-- start pre -->

33 teams entered, including GER (hosts). Winners of nine groups (five
groups of four teams and four groups of three teams) progressing to finals,
except Group 9, whose winner battled the best team of South American
Group 12.
Goal difference was used to separate teams, but teams level on it still
had a play-off game.
