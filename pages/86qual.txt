== Europe

four groups of five teams and wiiners three groups of four teams progress
into the finals. Originally the runners-up of four teams groups were to
play in a tournament (winner going into finals), but eventually FIFA's
Executive Commitee to arrange play-offs between runners-up of groups 1
and 5, and runner-up of group 7 and the Oceanian zone winner
