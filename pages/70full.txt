Mexico, 31 May to 21 June 1970 (Guadalajara, León, Mexico City, Puebla,
Toluca). Decided at FIFA Congress in Tokio, 8 Oct 1964. Entries deadline
15 Dec 1967. 75 teams entered (9 for the first time), 70 actually played.
Qualifications drawn 31 Jan 1968 in Casablanca. Finals drawn 10 Jan 1970
in Mexico City.
