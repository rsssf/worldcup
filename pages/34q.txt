== World Cup 1934 Qualifying

<!-- start pre -->
Hosts: Italy (not automatically qualified)
Holders: Uruguay (did not enter)

Group 1 [Sweden]
11.06.33  Stockholm       Sweden           6-2 Estonia
29.06.33  Kaunas          Lithuania        0-2 Sweden

Group 2 [Spain]
11.03.34  Madrid          Spain            9-0 Portugal
18.03.34  Lisboa          Portugal         1-2 Spain

Group 3 [Italy]
25.03.34  Milano          Italy            4-0 Greece
NB: both federations agreed to play a single match in Milano only

Group 4 [Hungary, Austria]
25.03.34  Sofia           Bulgaria         1-4 Hungary
25.04.34  Wien            Austria          6-1 Bulgaria
29.04.34  Budapest        Hungary          4-1 Bulgaria
Bulgaria withdrew

Group 5 [Czechoslovakia]
15.10.33  Warszawa        Poland           1-2 Czechoslovakia
15.03.34  Praha           Czechoslovakia   n/p Poland
NB: the Polish government prevented the Polish football team from travelling for the
    return leg for political reasons

Group 6 [Romania, Switzerland]
24.09.33  Beograd         Yugoslavia       2-2 Switzerland
29.10.33  Bern            Switzerland      2-2 Romania         [awarded 2-0 to Switzerland]
29.04.34  București       Romania          2-1 Yugoslavia

Group 7 [Netherlands, Belgium]
25.02.34  Dublin          Ireland          4-4 Belgium
08.04.34  Amsterdam       Netherlands      5-2 Ireland
29.04.34  Antwerpen       Belgium          2-4 Netherlands

Group 8 [Germany, France]
11.03.34  Luxembourg      Luxembourg       1-9 Germany
15.04.34  Luxembourg      Luxembourg       1-6 France

Group 9 [Brazil]
Peru withdrew, Brazil had a walkover.

Group 10 [Argentina]
Chile withdrew, Argentina had a walkover.

Group 11 [USA]
1st round
28.01.34  Port-au-Prince  Haiti            1-3 Cuba
01.02.34  Port-au-Prince  Haiti            1-1 Cuba
04.02.34  Port-au-Prince  Haiti            0-6 Cuba
2nd round
04.03.34  Cd. de México   Mexico           3-2 Cuba
11.03.34  Cd. de México   Mexico           5-0 Cuba
18.03.34  Cd. de México   Mexico           4-1 Cuba
3rd round
24.05.34  Roma            USA              4-2 Mexico

NB: see also ‹Cuba in the 1934 World Cup versus Haiti and Mexico, see page 34cubacampaign›

Group 12 [Egypt]
Turkey withdrew
16.03.34  Cairo           Egypt            7-1 Palestina
06.04.34  Tel-Aviv        Palestina        1-4 Egypt
