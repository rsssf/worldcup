France, 4-19 Jun 1938 (Antibes, Bordeaux, Le Havre, Lille, Marseille,
Paris, Reims, Strasbourg, Toulouse). Decided at FIFA Congress in Berlin
at 15 Aug 1936. Deadline entries 15 Feb 1937. 37 teams entered, 27
actually played (4 for the first time). ESP withdrew due to civil war.
Qualifications drawn 14 Mar 1937 at Paris, finals drawn 5 Mar 1938 at
Paris.
