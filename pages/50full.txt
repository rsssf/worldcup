Brasil, 24 June - 16 July 1950 (Belo Horizonte, Curitiba, Pórto Alegre,
Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo). Decided at FIFA Congress in Luxemburg
at 1 July 1946. Entries deadline 31 Dec 1948, 34 teams seeded, 25 actually
played (7 for the first time). TUR, IND, SCO waived their place in finals.
Qualiifications drawn 15 Jan 1949 in Geneve. Finals drawn 30 Apr 1950 in
London.
