France, 10 June - 12 July 1998 (Bordeaux, Lens, Lyon, Marseilles, Montpellier,
Nantes, Paris, St.Denis, St.Etienne, Toulouse). Decided by FIFA Executive
Committee 9 Jul 1992. Entries deadline 30 Sep 1995. 174 teams entered, 170
actually played (32 for the first time). Qualifications drawn 12 Dec 1995
in Paris. European play-offs drawn 13 Oct 1997 in Zürich. Finals drawn
4 Dec 1997 in Marseilles.
