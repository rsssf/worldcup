== Africa

17 teams entered, including SAF (later banned from FIFA for apartheid
issues) and CON (after deadline). The remaining 15 teams withdrew after
seeding due to FIFA reserving one place for both Africa and Asia.

== Asia

5 teams entered, including ISR and SYR seeded in Europe. SYR withdrew after
seeding solidarising with African countries. The entry of FIL was rejected
due to payment delays. KOR withdrew. The remaining team (KLD) played against
the only entered team from Oceania, AUS.

== Oceania

AUS entered as first country from that confederation, played against KLD
after other Asian countries resigned.
