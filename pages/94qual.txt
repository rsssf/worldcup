== Europe


39 teams entered, including GER (champions) and ISR. LIE withdrew before
seeding. JUG expulsed due to UN sanctions after seeding. Winners and
runners-up of six groups (five groups of six teams and one group of
seven teams) progress into the finals. CZE i SLK became separate
states on 1 Jan 1993, but put up an united team. RUS inherited the
rights of CIS, other CIS members were not admitted.
