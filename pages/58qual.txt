== Europe

<!-- start pre -->

31 teams entered, including GER (champions) and SWE (host). CYP and TUR
alloted to a mixed Euro-Afro-Asian zone, both withdrew after seeding.
Ten places for the winners of nine groups and one of the runners-up
after a play-off with ISR (see group 14)
For the first time teams were seeded according to their strengths, and
not geographically.

== Africa

3 teams entered, alloted to group 14 along with Asian teams and CYP and
TUR; EGY, SYR and SUD withdrew not wanting to play ISR. Because ISR would
qualify from the group without playing any game, FIFA decided to hold
a play-off with one of the runners-up of European groups, chosen by lot.
